How We Win

Rapid Prototyping

Makerspaces, workshops and industrial partners across the city and country can collaborate to create emergency supplies faster than traditional supply chains can respond.

Local Logistics

During an active disaster response, anyone with a bicycle, scooter or car can help deliver aid and supplies to the people who need it most.

Knowledge Sharing

Every disaster generates lessons learned, which we can capture and share to empower people in future situations. Check out our knowledge base.
